Cardio Workouts

1. Running

  • Warm up with a brisk walk or light jog.
  • Gradually increase your speed.
  • Maintain a steady pace for the duration of your run.
  • Cool down with a slow walk.

2. Jump Rope

  • Hold the jump rope handles at your sides.
  • Rotate your wrists to swing the rope over your head.
  • Jump over the rope as it comes towards your feet.
  • Continue for the desired number of reps or time.

3. Cycling

  • Start with a gentle warm-up.
  • Gradually increase your speed and resistance.
  • Maintain a steady pace or do intervals for a more intense workout.
  • Cool down by cycling at a slower pace.

4. High Knees

  • Stand with your feet hip-width apart.
  • Lift your knees up to your chest, alternating quickly.
  • Keep your core tight and back straight.
  • Continue for the desired time or reps.

5. Burpees

  • Start in a standing position.
  • Drop into a squat and place your hands on the floor.
  • Kick your feet back into a plank position.
  • Perform a push-up, then jump your feet back to your hands.
  • Jump up with your arms overhead.

6. Mountain Climbers

  • Start in a plank position.
  • Bring one knee towards your chest.
  • Switch legs quickly, as if running in place.
  • Keep your core tight and back straight.

7. Jumping Jacks

  • Stand with your feet together and arms at your sides.
  • Jump your feet out while raising your arms overhead.
  • Jump back to the starting position.
  • Continue for the desired number of reps or time.
